Reporting Light Exposure

Generate a CSV file containing the spectral power distribution of all of the measurements taken during the experiment.

Specification

The first field contains the wavelength in nanometers. The subsequent fields contain the spectral irradiance for each of the measurements taken during the experiment:

Field Required? Description
1 Yes Wavelength in nanometers
2 Yes First measurement spectral irradiance in µW/mW/W per mm²/cm²/m²
3 No Second measurement spectral irradiance in µW/mW/W per mm²/cm²/m²
N No Nth measurement spectral irradiance in µW/mW/W per mm²/cm²/m²
